About Me

Hello, I'm Ahmad Bilal, a 21-year-old Uni school student currently in the Final Year. I'm passionate about game design and have honed my skills using Unity 2D/3D Builder.

What I Do

My primary focus in game design revolves around **level design**, **blackouts**, and **layouts**. I love creating engaging and challenging game levels that captivate and immerse players in the gaming experience. Through careful planning and meticulous attention to detail, I aim to craft levels that provide both fun and meaningful gameplay experiences.

My Tools

I rely on Unity 3D Builder to bring my game design visions to life. With the powerful tools and features it offers, I can prototype, iterate, and refine game levels to ensure they meet the high standards I set for my work.

What I Don't Do

While I'm deeply committed to level design, blackouts, and layouts, my specialization doesn't extend to **environment art**. I prefer to concentrate on game design's structural and interactive aspects, leaving the aesthetic and artistic elements to other talented individuals.

Join Me on My Journey

I'm excited to share my game design journey with you. Explore my portfolio to see my projects and follow along as I continue to grow and develop my skills in the world of game design. If you have any questions or want to collaborate on a project, feel free to reach out. Let's create amazing gaming experiences together!
