About me

I am a Software Engineer and Unity Architect specializing in designing scalable systems, immersive AR/VR simulations, and applied Artificial Intelligence. With a portfolio of 15+ shipped mobile titles and complex enterprise applications, I build robust digital environments engineered for performance and engagement.
